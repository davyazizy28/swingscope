SwingScope is a personal, long-only decision-support tool — it organizes data and enforces a rule, it does not predict markets. Signals are not financial advice. Backtested figures use demo price history and cover the technical rules only; the sentiment layer runs live-forward and cannot be cleanly backtested on free data. Past performance never guarantees future results. Size every position so a stop-out costs no more than 1–2% of capital.
